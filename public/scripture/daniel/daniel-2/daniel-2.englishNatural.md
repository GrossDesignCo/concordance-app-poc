
You the king, over your bed, your thoughts came up, what will be after this, and the declarer of mysteries caused you to know what will be.

And I, not in wisdom that is in me from all the living, this mystery he has revealed to me, that not over wisdom which is in me, that if not over knowledge of the king this mystery not caused me to know.

You the king, were seeing and behold, an image great image this exceedingly its image standing opposite you and its appearance fearsome.
