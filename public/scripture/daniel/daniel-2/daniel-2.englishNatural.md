
You O king, your thoughts upon your bed came up, what will be after this, and the revealer of mysteries caused you to know what will be.

And I, not in wisdom that is in me more than all the living, this mystery has been revealed to me, but on account of that the interpretation to the king they might make known.

You O king, were seeing and behold, an image one great image that was great, and its brightness surpassing, standing before you, and its appearance fearsome.
