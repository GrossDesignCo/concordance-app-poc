In the year of three to the kingdom of He-Will-Establish (Jehoiakim), king of Praise (Judah), came Nebuchadnezzar, king of Confusion (Babylon), to Foundation-of-Peace (Jerusalem), and he besieged over it.

And the Lord gave into his hand, He-Will-Establish (Jehoiakim), king of Praise (Judah), and part of the vessels of the house of the God, and he brought them to Confusion (Babylon), to the land of Tooth-of-Two (Shinar), to the house of his God, and he brought the vessels to the house of treasure of his God.

And in all the matters of the wisdom of understanding, which the king sought from them, and he found them ten hands over all the mages, the astrologers, which were in all his kingdom.

And God-Is-My-Judge (Daniel) was, until the year of one to Cyrus the king.
