
vayHi khol-haAretz safah echat uDevarim achadim

vayHi beNosam miQedem 
vayYimtse'u biqah beEretz ShinAr 
vayYeshvu sham

vayYomeru ish el-reeyhu 
havah nilvnah levenim veNisrefah lisrefah 
vatTehi lahem haLevenah leEven 
veHacheymar hayah lahem laChomer

vayYomeru havah nivneh-lanu ir uMigdal 
veRosho baShamayim 
veNaaseh-lanu shem 
pen-naFuts al-peney kol-haAretz

vayYered YHWH liRot et-haIr 
veEt-haMigdal asher banu beney haAdam

vaYomer YHWH 
hen am echad 
veSafah achat leKhulam
veZeh hachilam laAsot 
veAttah lo-yibatser mehem kol asher yazmu laAsot

havah nerdah 
veNavlah sham sefatam
asher lo yishme'u ish sefat reehu

vayYafetz YHWH otam misham al-peney khol-haAretz
vayachdelu livnot haIr

al-ken qara shemah Babel
ki-sham balal YHWH sefat kol-haAretz
umisham hephitzam YHWH al-peney kol-haAretz
