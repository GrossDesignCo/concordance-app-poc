
uMoshe hayah roeh et-tzon Yitro chotno kohen Midyan 
vaYinhag et-haTzon achar haMidbar 
vaYavo el-har haElohim Chorevah

vaYera malakh YHWH elav beLabbat-esh miTokh haSeneh 
vaYar veHineh haSeneh boer baEsh vehaSeneh einenu ukkal

vaYomer Moshe asurah-nah veEreh et-haMareh haGadol haZeh madua lo-yivar haSeneh

vaYar YHWH ki sar lirot 
vaYiqra elav Elohim miTokh haSeneh vaYomer Moshe Moshe vaYomer hineni

vaYomer al-tiqrav halom 
shal-nealekha meAl raglekha ki haMaqom asher atah omed alav admat-qodesh hu

vaYomer anokhi elohei avikha elohei Avraham elohei Yitzchaq vElohei Yaaqov

vaYaster Moshe panav ki yare meHabit el-haElohim

vaYomer YHWH raoh raiti et-oni ammi asher beMitzrayim veEt-tzaaqatam shamati miPenei nogsav ki yadati et-makheovav

vaYomer Moshe el-haElohim hineh anokhi va el-benei Yisrael veAmarti lahem elohei avoteikhem shelachani aleikhem 
veAmru-li mah-shemo mah omar alehem

vaYomer Elohim el-Moshe 
ehyeh asher ehyeh 
vaYomer koh tomar livney Yisrael 
ehyeh shlachani aleikhem

vaYomer od Elohim el-Moshe 
koh-tomar el-beney Yisrael 
YHWH Elohey avoteikem 
Elohey Avraham 
Elohey Yitzchaq 
veElohey Yaaqov 
shlachani aleikhem 
zeh-shmi leOlam
